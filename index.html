Section 4 · Emission split
Who has received SN54 alpha emissions, and how was the split decided?
Answer: Under dTAO mechanics, MIID alpha emissions are distributed proportionally to stake weight across all registered hotkeys on SN54. The subnet owner controls the validator take (default 18%), applied to all delegated stake flowing through their hotkey. Miners receive the miner emission share (~41%) proportional to their Yuma consensus score. No confirmed third-party investor hotkey allocation (analogous to Yuma/DCG on SN44) has been identified for SN54. The seed investors appear to hold equity in Yanez Compliance rather than direct on-chain emission allocations.
